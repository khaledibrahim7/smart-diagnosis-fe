🚀 About Smart Diagnosis

Smart Diagnosis is an AI-powered medical assistant designed to provide instant symptom analysis and personalized health recommendations.

What is Smart Diagnosis?

AI-driven platform for quick and reliable symptom analysis, helping users understand their health conditions better.

Key Features

  • 🚀Fast & Accurate Diagnosis
  • 🌍Multi-language Support
  • 🔒Secure & Private
  • 💡AI-driven Suggestions
  • 📊Continuous Algorithm Improvement

Innovation & Uniqueness

Unlike traditional symptom checkers, Smart Diagnosis integrates real-time AI predictions, user-friendly interfaces, and adaptive learning models for better accuracy.

Scientific Research & Validation

Our AI model is trained on medical datasets, peer-reviewed research, and verified by healthcare professionals to ensure accuracy.

Real-World Applications

Smart Diagnosis can be used by individuals for self-assessment, clinics for preliminary screening, and telemedicine platforms for remote patient evaluation.

Ethical Considerations & Data Privacy

Technology Stack

Using AI, machine learning, and extensive medical databases, Smart Diagnosis is developed with Angular for frontend and Spring Boot for backend.
